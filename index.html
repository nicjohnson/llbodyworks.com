Ashiatsu / Barefoot Massage

  • Medium or Deep Pressure, depending on client’s preference
  • Therapist uses parallel bars hung from the ceiling to balance and maneuver, while administering massage with the feet
  • Pressure is broad, deep, consistent, and has powerful grounding and relaxing effects
  • This is a full body massage. Every area that can be massaged by the hands can be massaged by the feet
  • Available in 50, 80, or 110 minute sessions

What to expect: This modality is performed with the client disrobed, lying on the massage table beneath a massage towel (link). Therapist applies lotion with the hands, and then proceeds to massage with the feet. When client is face up, the limbs are modestly exposed at the same time for ease of working as the therapist massages the anterior side of the legs and arms. When face-up, the client’s eyes will be covered with a loose overlapping head wrap.

Thai Massage

  • Light, Medium, or Deep pressure, depending on client’s preference
  • Focus on muscle relaxation, joint range of motion, and flexibility
  • Client is clothed
  • Massage is performed on a mat on the floor

What to expect: Client should come in comfortable clothes that do not need frequent adjusting. This modality is performed with the client fully clothed, lying on a mat on the floor. Therapist will use compressive massage techniques with a rocking motion. Hands and feet may be used. They will also move client into stretches. Sen lines (Thai energy lines) will be addressed with compression and intention. Client may be worked in prone, supine, side-lying, and seated positions.
